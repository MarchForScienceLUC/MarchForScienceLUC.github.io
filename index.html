Ramblers March

Join us as we gear up to march for science Aprill 22nd.

Why We March

Recent events in public policy have put a spotlight on the scientific community, science, and their role within government, media, and the public. We march to bridge the distance between science and society. We march to showcase Chicago as The Science City. We march toward an ethical science. Now is the time to take a stand for science and the communities it affects.

Who We Are

We are students. We are scientists. We are enthusiasts. We are supporters. We are Chicagoans. We are Ramblers. Science is meaningful to all of us whether we are scientists ourselves, enthusiasts of science, or simply Chicagoans that value a method to uncover truth. We were motivated to become a guardian of science despite our differences. Our diversity strengthens us.

Where We March

Enter March programming at the intersection of Congress Parkway and Congress Plaza Drive. We will hold a rally just south of Jackson Drive (entrance from north will be blocked) starting at 10am, and march at 11am to the Museum campus where our Science in Chicago Expo will take place from 12-3pm.
